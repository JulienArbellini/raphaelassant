KAO-LIANG & BETEL NUTS
檳 榔 高 粱
KAO-LIANG & BETEL NUTS is an ongoing photography project started in march 2020 in Taiwan, depicting the vivid character and vibrant energy of the people and the area surrounding Longshan temple (龍山寺) in Wanhua district (萬華區). An aging and neglected neighborhood, historical and spiritual heart of Taipei city, where homeless people, sex workers, tea parlor hostesses, gangsters, flea market hawkers, monks, tourists and pensioners mix daily in a microcosm of ever-changing scenes.

About/Contact
Raphaël Assante is a French photographer currently based between Paris and Taipei city.
He grew up in the Corsican countryside where he spent most of his childhood outdoor playing in nature. His passion for photography sparked in 2019 in Taipei city, Taiwan, after a stranger offers him a film camera.
Practice of street photography awakened the same sensations he had as a kid while going on explorations of the neighbouring fields where he would spend hours observing, hunting, and collecting insects.
At present, Raphaël’s playground is urban, his curiosity people-oriented, and he captures and curates instants.
Raphaël shoots with flash and manual analogue film cameras, heavily influenced by photographers such as Mark Cohen, Bruce Gilden, Seiji Kurata, and Yang Seung-wo, his visual language emphasizes rawness, dynamism, and emotions.
拉斐爾·阿桑特 (Raphaël Assante) 是一位法國攝影師，目前居住在巴黎和台北兩座城市之間。
他在科西嘉鄉村長大，童年的大部分時間都在大自然中戶外玩耍。2019 年，一位陌生人向他提供了一台底片相機後，他在台灣台北市激發了對攝影的熱情。
街頭攝影的實踐喚醒了他小時候與大自然探索時所擁有的同樣的感覺，在那裡他會花幾個小時觀察、狩獵和收集昆蟲。
目前，拉斐爾的遊樂場是城市，他的好奇心集中在人身上，他捕捉並策劃瞬間。
拉斐爾使用閃光燈和手動底片相機進行拍攝，深受馬克·科恩 (Mark Cohen)、布魯斯·吉爾登 (Bruce Gilden)、倉田 精二 (Seiji Kurata) 和梁丞佑 (Yang Seung-Woo) 的影響，他的視覺語言強調原始、活力和情感。
